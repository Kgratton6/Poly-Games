Rules of Thirty-One

The purpose of the game is to collect cards that total 31 points with cards of the same color.

Card Values:

  • Aces: 11 points
  • Ten, Jack, Queen, King: 10 points each
  • Numbered cards: Face value (e.g., 2 = 2 points, 3 = 3 points, etc.)

Gameplay:

  • Each round, you draw a card from the deck or from the discard pile.
  • You must discard one of your cards after drawing.
  • You can only hold up to 3 cards in your hand at any time.

Winning :

  • If a player finishes their turn and reaches 31 points, they instantly win.
  • Before starting your turn, you can choose to end the game if you believe you are close enough to 31 points. If you do, skip your turn, and other players get one final round. Once it returns to you and you choose to end the game, all players reveal their cards, and the player with the highest point total wins.
