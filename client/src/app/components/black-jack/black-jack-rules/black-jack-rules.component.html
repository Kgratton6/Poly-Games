Rules of Blackjack

The goal of Blackjack is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand without exceeding 21.

Card Values:

  • Aces: 1 or 11 points (player's choice)
  • Face cards (King, Queen, Jack): 10 points each

Gameplay:

  • At the start, each player and the dealer are dealt two cards. Players' cards are usually dealt face up, while the dealer has one card face up and one face down.
  • Players take turns deciding to "Hit" (draw a card) or "Stand" (keep their current hand).
  • If a player’s hand value exceeds 21, they "bust" and lose the round.

Winning :

  • If a player's hand value is closer to 21 than the dealer’s without busting, the player wins.
  • If the dealer busts, all remaining players win.
  • If a player’s hand value and the dealer’s hand value are equal, it is a "push" and the player's bet is returned.
  • A "Blackjack" (an Ace and a 10-point card) is the highest possible hand and beats any other hand except another Blackjack.
